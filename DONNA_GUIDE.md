# Meet Donna — Your AI Chief of Staff

Donna helps you run your day. You talk to her in plain English, and she plans your
tasks, manages your calendar, remembers what matters to you, and reshuffles
everything the moment your day changes. Think of her less as a chatbot and more as a
sharp, warm personal secretary who actually *does* things for you.

This guide explains what Donna can do and how to use each part.

---

## Getting started

1. **Open Donna.** You can jump straight in with the **demo** — no sign-up needed —
   or sign in with Google/email to get your own private space.
2. **Tell Donna about yourself** (Settings, or the prompt on your home screen):
   your name, what you do, where, your working style, and your start/end times.
   The more she knows, the better she plans — and the less she has to guess.
3. **Start your day** from the **Today** screen, or just open **Chat** and talk.

---

## The five areas of Donna

Donna is organized into five places, each with a clear job:

### 🏠 Today — your day at a glance
Your home screen. Before you do anything, Donna shows you:
- A warm greeting and today's date.
- **At-a-glance stats:** how many tasks and events you have, and how much you've
  completed.
- **Your momentum:** a colorful streak grid of how productive you've been recently.
- **Today's schedule** (your timed events) and **Today's focus** (your to-dos).
- **Quick actions** to get a morning briefing, ask "what now?", or jump into chat.

Use this to orient yourself in five seconds: *"Here's what my day looks like."*

### 💬 Chat — talk to Donna
This is where the magic happens. Just type naturally and Donna understands what you
mean. A few things you can say:
- **Add tasks:** *"I need to finish my lab report, prep for the interview, and reply
  to Connor today."* → She lists them back, you confirm, and she saves them.
- **Schedule events:** *"Add a call with Connor next Monday at 3pm for an hour."* →
  It lands on your calendar.
- **Mark progress:** *"I finished the lab report."* → She checks it off.
- **Replan:** *"Something urgent came up — replan my day."* → She actually
  rearranges things (more on this below).
- **Ask for guidance:** *"What should I work on right now?"* → One clear answer.
- **Just talk:** *"I'm feeling overwhelmed."* → She responds like a person, not a form.

She replies as she types, and she formats smartly — bullet points when she's listing
things, normal sentences when she's talking with you.

### 📅 Calendar — your week, time-blocked
A clean weekly grid showing your events laid out by time, like a real calendar.
- See your whole week; jump to **Today** or flip between weeks.
- A live **"now" line** shows the current time.
- **Click any event to edit or delete it** — change the title, time, or remove it.
- **Three ways to fill it:** tell Donna in chat, **upload a screenshot** of a
  timetable (she reads it for you), or import an **.ics** file. You can also export
  your calendar to Apple/Google Calendar.
- If two things overlap, Donna **flags the conflict** instead of silently
  double-booking you.

### 📈 Productivity — where your time goes
Your personal insights page:
- A **contribution heatmap** (like the green squares on GitHub) showing how much you
  got done each day over the past weeks.
- Your **current streak**, **completion rate**, **focus hours**, and **best day**.
- A **"where your time goes"** breakdown by category, so you can see what's actually
  eating your week.
- Click any day to see exactly what happened that day.

### ⚙️ Settings — your profile & preferences
- Your **profile**: name, role, school/company, working style, and daily hours.
  This is what makes Donna feel personal and stops her from guessing.
- **Theme** (dark/light) and **notifications**.
- **What Donna remembers** — you can review the facts she's stored about you and
  remove anything you don't want kept.

## What makes Donna different

### She actually takes action
When you ask Donna to add a task, schedule a meeting, or cancel an event, she
**does it** — your tasks and calendar really change. She confirms before saving and
won't create duplicates, so you stay in control.

### She replans your day — and you can undo it
Tell her *"my afternoon just got blown up, replan,"* and Donna will reprioritize
what matters, push what can wait to tomorrow, and slot in the new urgent thing. She
then shows you a clear summary of **exactly what changed**, with a one-click
**Undo** if you don't like it.

### She remembers you
Donna keeps a profile of who you are and recalls relevant bits of your past
conversations, so she gives advice that fits *you* — your goals, your hours, your
style — instead of generic suggestions. You're always in control of what she
remembers.

### She's proactive
Donna can give you a **morning briefing** to start your day and an **end-of-day
wrap** to close it out, and she can send **reminders** before your events — so you're
never caught off guard.

## A typical day with Donna

- **Morning:** Open **Today**, glance at your schedule and momentum, and ask Donna for
  your **morning briefing**.
- **During the day:** Drop tasks and meetings into **Chat** as they come up. Mark
  things done as you finish them. Check the **Calendar** to see your time-blocks.
- **When plans change:** Ask Donna to **replan** — she handles the reshuffle.
- **Evening:** Get your **end-of-day wrap** to see what you accomplished and what
  rolls to tomorrow.
- **Anytime:** Visit **Productivity** to see your streak and where your time went.

---

## Tips for getting the most out of Donna

- **Fill in your profile first.** It's the single biggest thing that makes Donna feel
  like *yours*.
- **Talk to her naturally.** You don't need special commands — say it how you'd say
  it to a person.
- **Confirm when she asks.** For anything she saves, she'll check with you first.
- **Use replan when your day shifts.** It's faster than rearranging things by hand —
  and you can always undo.
- **Upload screenshots of your timetable** instead of typing every class — she'll
  read them for you.
